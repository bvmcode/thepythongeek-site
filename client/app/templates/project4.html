About the Model

I developed a machine learning model to predict the daily high temperature (°F) for my location in Atco, NJ. This page displays the comparison between the model's prediction and the observed high temperature from my weather station. The model input is derived from surrounding NWS 12Z sounding data as well as 12Z observations from my station.
